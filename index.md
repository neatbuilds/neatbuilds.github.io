## Apps

### PingReply

Auto-reply to missed calls with SMS. No internet, no accounts, no data collection.

- Jetpack Compose + Material 3
- Room database, DataStore preferences
- Foreground service with phone number validation (libphonenumber)
- Trilingual: English, Portuguese, Spanish
